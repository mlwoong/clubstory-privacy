This Privacy Policy explains how "Club Story: Baseball" ("the App") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. By installing or using the App, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Information We Collect

The App offers two usage modes:

  • Guest mode: Use the App immediately without signing up or logging in. Gameplay data (save files, players/teams, settings) is stored locally on your device. Additional data (profile, attendance, club points) is stored in a separate on-device key-value store (SharedPreferences) and is not transmitted to any external server.
  • Google Sign-In mode (optional): If you choose to sign in with your Google account, the data listed below is stored in the cloud (Google Cloud Firestore) so that certain data can be preserved across device changes or reinstallation.

1-1. Data Collected When Signed in with Google (optional)

Data CollectedWhenProcessorPurpose
Email address, display name (Google profile name), profile photo URL, Google account unique identifier (UID) When you sign in with Google Google Firebase Authentication Account identification and login
Club points balance, lifetime earned/spent history, account creation & last login timestamps During gameplay after signing in Google Cloud Firestore Restoring club points after device change or reinstallation
Daily attendance counter (0–4), last attendance date When daily attendance reward is claimed Google Cloud Firestore Preventing duplicate claims and tracking 5-day cycle progress

None of the above is collected if you choose "Continue as Guest". Google Sign-In is purely optional; core App features are available in Guest mode.

1-2. User-Provided Information (optional, new in v1.0.2)

Data CollectedWhenStoragePurpose
Username (display name) — up to 20 characters width (10 Korean / 20 English) My Account → Profile Edit Google: Firestore / Guest: On-device Personal identifier within the App
Avatar — choice of 15 built-in avatars OR gallery image (optional) My Account → Profile Edit Built-in avatar: synced to Google / Gallery image: device only Visual self-representation within the App

Only information you explicitly enter is collected. Gallery images you select are copied only to a private app folder on your device and are never transmitted to Google Cloud or any external server. Because the username may be displayed to other users when ranking features are added in the future, please avoid using your real name, contact details, or sensitive personal information.

1-3. Automatically Collected Data (all users)

We do not collect phone numbers, location data, contacts, microphone, SMS, physical activity, or any other data beyond the items listed above. However, for the optional gallery image feature we require the media permission (Android 13+ READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, iOS Photos), which only operates after you grant permission via the system dialog.

2. How We Use Information

  • Google account info: account identification, login processing, cross-device synchronization
  • Club points data: restoring accumulated rewards after device change or reinstallation
  • Daily attendance records: preventing duplicate daily reward claims and tracking cycle progress
  • Username and avatar: in-app self-identification
  • Crash reports: analyzing crash and error causes, fixing bugs, improving stability
  • Installation ID: deduplicating crash reports, identifying issues on specific devices or OS versions

Collected data is not used for advertising, marketing, sale to third parties, profiling, or any purpose other than those listed above.

3. Storage & Security

3-1. Storage Locations & Retention

CategoryStorage LocationRetention Period
Google account info / Club points / Attendance records / Profile (username, built-in avatar index) Google Cloud Firestore (operated by Google LLC) Retained until you delete your account; deleted immediately upon account deletion
Username unique collection (lowercase name → your UID mapping) Google Cloud Firestore (usernames) Released immediately upon name change or account deletion (other users may reclaim the name)

Gameplay data (players, teams, season, etc.) Local SQLite on your device Retained until you uninstall the App; cleared immediately upon uninstall

3-2. Security Measures

  • Transmission Security: All transmissions are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Access Control: Firestore Security Rules restrict each user to accessing only their own account data. The username unique collection (usernames) is readable by all users for duplicate check purposes, but writable only by the owner. The App developer can view only aggregated statistics via the Firebase console and does not arbitrarily access individual user gameplay data.
  • Storage Security: Google Cloud Firestore data is encrypted at rest.
  • Local Images: Gallery-selected avatar images are stored only in the app's private folder and cannot be accessed by other apps.

6. User Rights

You may exercise the following rights regarding your personal data:

6-1. In-App Actions

  • Profile Edit: [Menu > My Account > Profile Edit] lets you freely change your username and avatar. Username may be changed once every 7 days; the previous name is released immediately and may be claimed by other users.
  • Sign Out: [Menu > My Account > Sign Out] disconnects your Google account at any time. Your gameplay data on the device is preserved.
  • Delete Account (permanent): [Menu > My Account > Delete Account] immediately and permanently deletes all cloud data linked to your Google account (club points, attendance records, profile, username unique mapping, etc.). This action cannot be undone.
  • Reset Guest Data: [Menu > My Account > Reset Data] immediately deletes all local Guest-mode data (club points, attendance, profile).
  • Uninstall the App: Uninstalling the App removes all on-device gameplay data and local avatar images. (Cloud data is removed only via the "Delete Account" action.)

6-2. Email Requests

  • To request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your information, please contact us at the email below.
  • To request deletion of crash reports already sent to Firebase, please contact us at the email above. Because the data contains no personal identifiers, separating an individual user's records from aggregated reports may be technically limited.
  • If you do not agree with Firebase services' data processing, please use Guest mode only or uninstall the App.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 14. If a guardian discovers that personal information of a child under 14 has been collected, please contact us at the email above and we will delete it immediately.
