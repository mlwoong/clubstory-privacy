This Privacy Policy explains how "Club Story: Baseball" ("the App") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. By installing or using the App, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require sign-up or login. All gameplay data (save files, player and team information, settings) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external server.

For stability monitoring and bug diagnosis, the following information may be collected automatically:

Data CollectedWhenProcessorPurpose
Crash reports (stack trace, memory state at crash, OS version, device model, app version, locale) When the app crashes Google Firebase Crashlytics Bug diagnosis & stability improvement
Anonymous installation ID (Firebase Installation ID) On first launch Google Firebase Deduplicating crash reports & estimating affected user count

None of this information personally identifies you. We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, location data, contacts, photos, or account information.

2. How We Use Information

  • To analyze the cause of app crashes and errors
  • To fix bugs and improve stability
  • To identify problems on specific devices or OS versions

Collected data is not used for advertising, marketing, sale to third parties, or any purpose other than those listed above.

3. Storage & Security

  • Storage Location: Google Firebase servers (operated by Google LLC)
  • Retention Period: Crash reports are retained for up to 90 days per Firebase Crashlytics policy, then deleted automatically.
  • Access: Only the App developer can view aggregated statistics through the Firebase console.
  • Transmission Security: All transmissions are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

4. Third-Party Sharing & Processors

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties. The following processor handles data on our behalf for the purpose described:

ProcessorPurposeData Processed
Google LLC (Firebase Crashlytics) Error analytics and crash reporting Crash stack, device info, anonymous installation ID

Google's data processing policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Advertising & In-App Purchases

The current (beta) version of the App does not contain advertisements or in-app purchases. Should advertising (e.g., Google AdMob) or in-app purchases (Google Play Billing) be introduced in a future production release, this Privacy Policy will be updated and users notified through an in-app announcement.

6. User Rights

  • You may stop further data collection at any time by uninstalling the App from your device.
  • If you wish to request deletion of crash reports already sent to Firebase, please contact us at the email above. Because the data contains no personal identifiers, separating an individual user's records from aggregated reports may be technically limited.
  • If you do not agree with Firebase Crashlytics' data processing, please do not use the App or uninstall it.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 14. If a guardian discovers that personal information of a child under 14 has been collected, please contact us at the email above and we will delete it immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated due to changes in applicable laws, the App's services, or our security practices. Material changes will be communicated through an in-app announcement or by updating the "Last Updated" date on this page.
