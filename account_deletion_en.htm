Club Story: Baseball — Account & Data Deletion

App Name: Club Story: Baseball
Package ID: app.clubstory.baseball
Developer: Independent Developer (mlwoong85@gmail.com)
Last Updated: May 25, 2026

This page explains how users of "Club Story: Baseball" can request deletion of their account and associated data. In compliance with Google Play policy, we provide two ways to delete your account — one inside the App and one from outside the App.

Option 1. In-App Deletion (recommended)

If you still have the App installed and are signed in with Google, you can permanently delete your account and all cloud data immediately using the steps below.

  1. Open the App → top-right Menu (☰) button
  2. Select "My Account"
  3. Tap "Delete Account" at the bottom of the screen
  4. Confirm by tapping "Delete Permanently" in the warning dialog

Deletion is processed immediately with no waiting period or additional verification step.

Option 2. Email Request (if the App has been uninstalled)

If you have already uninstalled the App or cannot access your device, please email us to request deletion.

📧 To: mlwoong85@gmail.com
📌 Subject: [Account Deletion Request] Club Story: Baseball
📝 Please include:
  • The email address you used for Google Sign-In
  • Reason (optional, may be omitted)

Processing time: We will process the request and reply within 7 business days.

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is a permanent, irreversible action. Even if you sign in again with the same Google account, your previous club points and history cannot be recovered.

Guest Mode Users

If you have only used the App in Guest mode (without Google Sign-In), all your data is stored exclusively on your device. Uninstalling the App will automatically erase all data, and no separate deletion request is needed.
